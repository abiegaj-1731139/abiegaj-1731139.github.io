Design

A timeless design that is both sporty and elegant

The exterior and interior surfacing of the s2000 was at least 5 years ahead of its time at its debut. It combined sharp sporty lines with elegantly simple surfacing, a combination utterly lacking in modern day cars

From the way the arches seemed to wrap around the front wheel, a design cue aston martin took inspiration from for their vantage line. Or the way the profile seems to be so low and sleek, almost tangent with the top of the tires..
